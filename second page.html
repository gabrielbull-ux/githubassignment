History of Remika cakes

Remika cakes was founded in the middle of 2020 , inspired by the creativity and bond between two sisters,Korto and Anita. The name "Remika" combines the names of their daughters Remma and anika,reflecting the personal tpuch and family-driven motivation behind the business. Initially,with little knowledge of cake design, the founders embraced their passion for inclusiveness and artistic expression, leading them to establish the business.

To refine their skills, they attended various training programs and classes in multiple countries,including Nigeria and Ghana, where they gained exposure to diverse cake making techniques. This global experience has played a key role in shaping the distinctive styles and flavors of Remika Cakes.

Since its inception, Remika Cakes has become known for its dedication to providing cakes that cater to all occasions, with focus on inclusivity, beautifully crafted cake, no matter the design or dietary needs.
